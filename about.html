GPA

GPA stands for Grade Point Average, which is a measure of academic performance in Anna University. GPA is calculated as the ratio of the sum of the products of the number of credits of courses registered and the grade points scored in those courses to the sum of the number of credits in the semester1. GPA can range from 0 to 10, with higher values indicating better performance.



CGPA

CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average, which is a measure of the overall academic performance in Anna University. CGPA is calculated as the ratio of the sum of the products of the number of credits of courses registered and the grade points scored in those courses to the sum of the number of credits in all the semesters. CGPA can range from 0 to 10, with higher values indicating better performance.
